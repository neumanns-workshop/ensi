Type G Variant (79-7-8, 77 rev. 19-21)

Conjuration:"If a man had a bad dream and is depressed, he shall tell (pasaru) the dream to a tartutu (reed), burn it in fire, and blow into the fire himself. He will be relieved."

Additional Ritual: In the morning, the man shall anoint his hand with perfumed oil, and he will be relieved.
