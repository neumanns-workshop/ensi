Type L (KAR 252 IV: 8-14)

Conjuration:"Evildoer, cut-throat, leader of the dark powers, full of foolishness, leader of hidden evil—day and night—Zaggarra (God of Dreams), turn the evil you have done into blessing, [goddess ...] turn whatever you have done into blessing! Be gracious, Utu, may he make favorable the [ ], be gracious, Marduk!"

Ritual: This conjuration shall be recited over a taksiru (bandage or phylactery), which shall then be placed around the neck of the person fearing evil dreams.
