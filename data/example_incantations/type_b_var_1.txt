
Type B Variant (KAR 252 III: 47-51)

Conjuration:"Dispel, remove the curse, ... dog! May the wind carry off the evil of the dream I have had!"

Title: A prayer to remove the evil consequences of an evil dream.

Ritual: When a man has a dream, he shall libate vinegar before setting his foot on the floor. Then, he shall set his foot on the floor, and the evil of the dream will be removed from him.
