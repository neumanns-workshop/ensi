Type M (KAR 252 IV: 38-40)

Conjuration:"My gracious god—stand at my side! My preeminent god—shine forth! My friendly god—listen to me! God Mamu of the dreams, my god, send (create) a favorable message!"

Ritual:

The conjuration shall be recited over an object (broken in text).

A plant called KUR.KUR shall be bound into the garment of the sleeper as a preventive measure.
