
Type B (KAR 252 III: 4-17)

Prayer to Utu: "Utu, you are the judge—judge my case! You are the one who makes decisions—decide my case! Change the dream I had into a good one! May I walk the straight road, may I have a companion! Utu, all day may they be good for me, Utu, all month may they be good for me!"

Title: A ritual preparation to remove the consequences of evil dreams. He shall recite this prayer with uplifted hands before Utu, and he will be relieved.
