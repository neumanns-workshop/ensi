A Dream of The Queen (KUB XV 3 I: 17-20)

"A dream of the queen: Somebody said again and again to me in a dream: 'Make a vow to the goddess Ningal as follows: "If that (disease) Fire-of-the-Feet of His Majesty will pass quickly, I shall make for Ningal ten(?) talla (oil-flasks) of gold set with lapis lazuli!"'"
