Another Dream of Hattushili (op. cit., p. 46, KBo VI 29 1: 16-21)

"Puduhepa, however, was a maid-servant of the Ishtar of the town Lawazantia and the daughter of Pentipsarri, the priest of Ishtar. And I did not marry her out of my own will(?); I took her upon the command of the goddess. The goddess entrusted her to me in a dream."

Omens:
A person dreams of being commanded by the goddess Ishtar.
A person dreams of the goddess entrusting someone to them.
A person dreams of marrying or being given a companion through divine command.
A person dreams of divine influence directing their life choices.
