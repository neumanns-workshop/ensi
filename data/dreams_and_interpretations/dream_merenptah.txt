Dream of Merenptah (W. M. Muller, Egypt. Res. I pl. 22, lines 28-29)

Then his majesty saw in a dream as if it were the image of Ptah standing in the presence of the Pharaoh, (and) he was as high as... And he said to him:

"Take now," (giving to him the sword), "and banish from yourself your troubled heart!"

(And) Pharaoh said to him: "Indeed..."
